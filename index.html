Compliance Framework
Applicable Standards
Standard / Code Scope Applicable Modules Status
ISO 45001:2018
 Occupational Health & Safety Management System — all hotel operations, contractor management, incident reporting, and emergency planning. HIRA, PTW, First Aid, Ergonomics, Chemical, Fire Mandatory
ISO 14001:2015
 Environmental Management System — waste management, energy use, water conservation, and environmental legal register. Environmental Management Mandatory
ISO 9001:2015
 Quality Management — SOPs, document control, customer safety, and continual improvement across all departments. Food Safety, All Modules Mandatory
NFPA 1 / NFPA 101
 Fire Prevention Code and Life Safety Code — building egress, occupancy classifications, fire protection systems in hotel high-rises. Fire Safety, Emergency Response Mandatory
NFPA 72
 National Fire Alarm & Signalling Code — fire detection, alarm systems, notification appliances and emergency communication systems. Fire Safety Required
Dubai Civil Defence
 DCD Fire & Life Safety Code of Practice — fire safety systems approval, occupancy permits, warden training, drills, and emergency plans. Fire Safety, Emergency Response, First Aid Mandatory
Dubai Municipality
 Food safety, waste management, swimming pool standards, pest control, environmental compliance for hospitality sector in Dubai. Food Safety, Environmental, Pool & Spa Mandatory
OSHAD-SF
 Abu Dhabi/UAE OSH System Framework — hazard identification methodology, contractor management, and incident investigation framework. HIRA, PTW, All Modules Best Practice
